Attractions

Whether guests are yearning for an action-packed adventure or just want to chill out in the fresh ocean breeze, the South West Bay of Tasmania offers an array of activities and attractions for guests of all interests.

Within 30 minutes

Margate Train

Tasmania’s last passenger train has its carriages of fun to browse – antiques and collectables; books, barber, gifts, lollie shop, pancake café, picnic and kids play area.

Mt. Wellington

Like a mother to Hobartians and hosting an array of walking tracks, helicopter rides and spectacular views

Taroona Shot tower

Built in 1870, lead shot was made by dropping molten lead from a great height. Explore the Tower to see how they did it.

Blackmans Bay Beach

A beautiful sheltered bay with a local town to explore and get some lunch after a walk on the 700 m wide beach.

Day Trips

Hastings Caves and Thermal Springs

Absolutely huge net work of tastefully lit caves to explore followed by a dip in the thermal springs!

Historic Richmond Town

With Australia’s oldest stone bridge in a beautiful setting with lots of local attractions.

Zoodoo Animal Safari Park

Drive around the park and meet native and exotic animals such as tigers and lions. With picnic and other facilities on site.

Bruny Island

Take the car on the ferry to beautiful Bruny Island and cruise the historical and scenic sites and taste the local gourmet food
